01 — Our Story

Born from a love
of good taste

Zest was born from a simple belief — every meal should be an experience, and every visit should feel like coming home. Nestled in the heart of Vapi, we've crafted a space where bold flavours meet refined comfort.

From cold brews steeped overnight to hand-kneaded pizzas baked stone-hot, every detail speaks of care, passion, and a relentless pursuit of the extraordinary.
